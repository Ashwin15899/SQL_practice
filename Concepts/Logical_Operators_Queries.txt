3.WAQTD DETAILS OF THE EMP ALONG WITH ANNUAL SALARY IF THEY ARE WORKING IN DEPT 30 AS SALESMAN AND 
  THEIR ANNUAL SALARY HAS TO BE GREATER THAN 14000.

  from emp 
  where job = 'salesman' and deptno=30 and sal*12>14000;
